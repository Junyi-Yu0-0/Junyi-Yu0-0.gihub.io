Benefits of Video Games

Video game can help us gain different skills.

1. Logic sence -Like playing "Angry Bird" or puzzles solving game, we can trains our brain to creative ideas for planing

2. Coordination and reaction -In shooting games we need to coordination with hand and eyes, also some time for online. we also need the coordination with teammate.

3.Spatial skills -A lot of game in this day have using math and imagination. Like "Portal" it let you using math think of lot of way to obtain the goal point.

4.Management and logistics -Some of the moblie games have to manage resources that are limited, for some of the game you need to build and upgrade the city for faster resouces income.

5.Multitasking -Strategy games forces player to be flexible react to unexpected surprise.

6.Quick thinking and decision -In games palyer have to make quick decision, because no one will wait for the next move you make. No only about timing, also you have to make them correct and accurate.

7.Anticipation and goal -In all type of game, we have to think of what is the next move to make, in order keeping long term goals, and that will make the game Interesting but also a sense of accomplishment for yourself.

8.Perseverance -In action game you will die and respawn, unlike real life. So you can keep trying to get better or the time.

9.Concentration -All the game, it need a lot of focus on the target and the goal that you want or have to make. Also, improve their reading scores by helping them concentrate.

10. Simulation -There is a lot of game today have simulation of all type of thing or occupation for real life. Job you never did in real life, example: a car repairer, computer builder, surgery doctor, chef and so many other job or skin.
