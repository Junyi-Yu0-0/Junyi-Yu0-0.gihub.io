Benefits of Video Games

Video game can help us gain different skills.

1. Logic sence -Like playing "Angry Bird" or puzzles solving games, we can train our brain to creative ideas for planning.

2. Coordination and reaction -In shooting games, we need to coordinate with hand and eyes, also some time for online. we also need coordination with teammates.

3.Spatial skills -A lot of games on this day have used math and imagination. Like "Portal" it let you using math think of a lot of ways to obtain the goal point.

4.Management and logistics -Some of the mobile games have to manage resources that are limited, for some of the games you need to build and upgrade the city for faster resources income.

5.Multitasking -Strategy games forces player to be flexible react to unexpected surprise.

6.Quick thinking and decision -In the games, players have to make a quick decision, because no one will wait for the next move you make. No only about timing, also you have to make the correct and accurate.

7.Anticipation and goal -In all types of games, we have to think of what is the next move to make, in order to keep long term goals, and that will make the game Interesting but also a sense of accomplishment for yourself.

8.Perseverance -In the action games, you will die and respawn, unlike real life. So you can keep trying to get better or the time.

9.Concentration -All the game, it needs a lot of focus on the target and the goal that you want or have to make. Also, improve their reading scores by helping them concentrate.

10. Simulation -There is a lot of game today have the simulation of all type of thing or occupation for real life. The job you never did in real life, for example, a car repairer, computer builder, surgery doctor, chef, and so many other jobs or skills.
